Who is Youssef GUBA?

Senior Mobile Developer
Educator. Community Maker.

I was the kid who couldn't stop asking "how does this actually work?" That curiosity never left — it just turned into a career dedicated to building scalable mobile solutions and empowering the next generation of developers.

Over 6+ years of professional experience, I've evolved from tinkering with code to architecting production-grade mobile products serving hundreds of thousands of users across the MENA region. As Senior Flutter Developer at Abwaab, I've led cross-functional teams in delivering high-impact edtech applications and guided mobile development best practices for organizations ranging from startups to enterprise companies.

But building apps is only half the story. The other half is people. I've mentored and trained 200+ developers through 1:1 mentorship sessions, monthly coaching programs, and public speaking engagements — not just teaching syntax, but fostering engineering thinking, building confidence, and helping them navigate career transitions. Watching someone achieve their technical goals is the most rewarding aspect of my work.

My technical expertise includes Flutter and Dart development, mobile app architecture, state management patterns, testing strategies, and responsive UI/UX design. I'm also fascinated by AI-powered products — building intelligent tools that seamlessly blend smart experiences with beautiful interfaces. The intersection of artificial intelligence and mobile development represents the frontier of user-centric technology.
